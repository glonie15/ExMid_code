Welcome to Philippine Crop Recommendation System

About This System

Our Crop Recommendation System helps farmers and gardeners in the Philippines make informed decisions about which crops to grow based on their soil conditions. By analyzing key soil parameters, we provide personalized recommendations for optimal crop selection.

Soil Analysis

Input your soil's pH level, type, and moisture content for accurate recommendations.

Crop Matching

Get personalized crop suggestions that thrive in your specific soil conditions.

Growing Timeline

Learn about planting seasons, duration, and expected harvest dates.

Growing Tips

Receive valuable advice on soil management and crop care.

How to Use

1

Measure Soil pH
